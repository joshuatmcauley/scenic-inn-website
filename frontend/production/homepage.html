Welcome to The Scenic Inn

A family-run Bar and Restaurant situated near Armoy and the iconic Dark Hedges. We offer a wide range of dishes including our famous Sizzling Pepper Chicken and locally sourced steaks.

We are passionate about local produce and fresh cooking, offering Lunch time, Evening, and Sunday Lunch menus. We cater for parties and can create tailor-made menus for your special occasions.

Enjoy live entertainment on weekends with free admission and different bands weekly.

LOCAL AWARD-WINNING BAR AND RESTAURANT

Since January 2013 the Scenic Inn has helped raise over ¬£200,000 for various charities in Northern Ireland, demonstrating our commitment to the local community.

In 2017 we won the very prestigious accolade 'Pub Food of The Year' for the whole of Northern Ireland along with Community Bar of the year.
