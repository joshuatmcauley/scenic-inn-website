Welcome to The Scenic Inn

We are a family run Bar and Restaurant situated just outside Armoy, a short distance from the Famous Dark Hedges. We offer a wide range of dishes to suit everyone's taste from our Famous Sizzling Pepper Chicken to the finest steaks which are all locally sourced.

We are Passionate about sourcing all of our produce locally and all of our food is cooked from fresh. We have a variety of menu's including Lunch time menu, Evening menu and Sunday Lunch. We can cater for parties and tailor menus to suit your needs. We offer live entertainment at the weekends with free admission, with a different band playing each week.
